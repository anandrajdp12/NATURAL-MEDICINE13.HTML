Natural Medicine



Natural medicine can also be referred to as naturopathy; it is a form of alternative medicine which involves homeopathy, herbalism,
acupuncture, diet and lifestyle counselling, and more.

It is thought that naturopathy originates back to 400BC and its founding principles were discovered by the Greek philosopher Hippocrates.

People who practise natural medicine are known as naturopathic doctors,
many of these practitioners will have obtained professional qualifications and have passed an examination to receive a license.
